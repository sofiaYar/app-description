Mine Sweeper

Welcome to the Mine Sweeper app! Challenge yourself with this classic puzzle game.

Features:

  • Easy, medium, and hard difficulty levels
  • Customizable game board size
  • Timer to track your game completion time
  • Flagging functionality to mark potential mines
  • High scores leaderboard
  • Interactive gameplay with intuitive touch controls

How to Play:

  1. Select a difficulty level to begin the game.
  2. Tap or click on a cell to reveal its content.
  3. Numbers on the revealed cells indicate the number of adjacent mines.
  4. Avoid tapping on cells with mines, or the game will end.
  5. Use long-press or right-click to flag potential mine locations.
  6. Continue uncovering cells and flagging mines until you clear the board or uncover a mine.
  7. Challenge yourself to complete the game in the shortest time possible!
